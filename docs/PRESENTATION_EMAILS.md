with crude −7%. So the turnarounds bite harder than the outage count alone suggests, into winter demand.

Bottom line: a heavier-than-last-year, Gulf-led turnaround season landing on a thin distillate cushion. Risk windows
are the Q1 freeze and the fall overlap.
